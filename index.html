My Bio Section

Hello everybody, my name is Kyle. I'm 35, yes that means I'm an 80's kid deal with it. I was a military brat, I joined the Army straight out of high school as well. I've lived all over the world and had many, MANY adventures. Not all of which were fun, but all of which were worth it. I started off as a crewchief on Chinooks a for the first five years of my career. I was stationed in Giebelstadt, Germany then Fort Riley, Kansas with the Big Red One (1st ID). We all piled onto the short bus for a field trip to Tikrit, Iraq. When I returned stateside I transferred to Military Intellegence(such a thing does not exist). I had a lovely vacation in balmy Monterey, Ca learning Arabic before being forced on to Augusta, Ga.


After the military I came back home to Knoxville, TN and took on work doing heavy aircraft maintenance for ExpressJet Airlines. I topped out in that industry for an aviation mechanic. To make any more I would have to take a higher position and move from the Knoxville area, and my family is here. After eight years, covid eventually hit the airlines & caused the company to downsize, our entire hangar in Knox was closed down. Very few airlines have been hiring since, and I believe software engineering & data science are the future of first-world economies. My ultimate goal is a bachelor's and disgustingly high paying job at Y-12 National Laboratories. Uncle Sam is highly sus, but he's sure got deep pockets!
